Learn PDPA + DNC with Phoebe

Two tracks from "what does the PDPA want" to a marketing campaign you can defend

Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act sets ten obligations for handling personal data - and a Do Not Call layer on top that governs every marketing call, text, and fax. A leader track teaches what the law requires and what it costs to get wrong, no jargon. A practitioner track turns it operational: check the registry, capture real consent, run a campaign that survives an audit, and respond to a breach. The running case is Marina Retail, a Singapore SME. Taught from the PDPC's own guidelines.
